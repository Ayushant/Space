Anveshak

A rover which has latest technology and advanced softwared to achive following aims: • Sustainable Infrastructure Development • Medical Research and Testing • Advanced Scientific Experiments • Environmental Monitoring • Bio-Regenerative Life Support Systems • Agricultural Testing • Interplanetary Internet

In Detail: • Sustainable Infrastructure Development: Rovers can survey and map potential sites for future infrastructure, identifying suitable locations for habitats and other structures. • Advanced Scientific Experiments: Rovers can carry out experiments related to soil analysis, atmospheric measurements, and radiation levels, providing valuable data for future missions. • Bio-Regenerative Life Support Systems: While rovers can't develop these systems, they can test environmental conditions and identify potential resources needed for bio-regenerative life support systems. • Medical Research and Testing: Rovers can collect data on environmental conditions that affect human health, such as radiation levels and dust composition. • Environmental Monitoring: Rovers can continuously monitor and report on environmental conditions, helping to assess the impact of human activities on extraterrestrial surfaces. • Agricultural Testing: Rovers can conduct preliminary tests on soil and environmental conditions to determine the feasibility of growing food on other planets.
